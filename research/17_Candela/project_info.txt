Project Name
Concrete Shell Typologies: A Catalogue of Structural Legacies of Félix Candela

2016
Tongji University

Description: This Master's thesis research is a comprehensive exploration of Félix Candela’s architectural legacy, with a particular focus on the structural aspects that underpinned his innovative designs. By closely examining Candela’s body of work, we seek to unveil the fundamental principles that informed his design process. Candela’s structural thinking was a driving force behind his architectural creations, and this study aims to shed light on the intricate relationship between form and structure in his designs. Through a meticulous examination of Candela’s structural philosophy, this research seeks to provide valuable insights for architects, engineers, and scholars interested in the intersection of structure and design. By gaining a deeper understanding of Candela’s principles and their impact on architectural creativity, we can better appreciate the enduring relevance of his contributions to the field.

Keywords
Structure, Architecture
