Project Name
Kinetic Stairs

2019
Cornell University

Team
Yao Lu, Yang Yang

Description: Developed in the Kinetic Facade seminar at Cornell University, instructed by Martin Miller, this project investigates the kinematics of adaptive systems and explores how a limited number of degrees of freedom can be used to drive larger and more complex kinetic behaviors. Through a series of small physical prototypes, the project examines the relationship between mechanical linkages, motion control, and architectural responsiveness. Arduino development boards were used to actuate the systems, allowing the prototypes to translate computational input into controlled physical movement.

Keywords
Kinematics, Structure
