Project Name
SwitchLink

2016
Tongji University

Description: This project is a wall switch attachment that adds infrared remote control capability to conventional wall switches without replacing the existing electrical hardware. Enclosed in a transparent acrylic housing, the device uses a compact mechanical linkage, servo motor, and microcontroller to physically toggle the switch in response to an infrared signal. The design provides a simple retrofit solution for improving convenience and accessibility while preserving the original switch and wiring. By combining digital control with a direct mechanical interface, the project explores how everyday architectural elements can be upgraded through low cost, modular, and non invasive computational devices.
