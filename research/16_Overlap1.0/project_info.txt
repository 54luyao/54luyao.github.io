Project Name
Overlap 1.0: A Self-Overlapping Shell

2016
Tongji University

Description: This design research project was developed during the DigitalFUTURES 2016 workshop, instructed by Philippe Block and Shajay Bhooshan. The workshop introduced the principles of 2D graphic statics and their extension into three-dimensional form-finding through thrust network analysis (TNA). Using the Rhino plugin RhinoVAULT, the project explores how equilibrium-based design methods can generate geometrically complex, structurally informed, and architecturally inspiring forms. Through this process, structural logic becomes an active driver of spatial and formal exploration.

Keywords
Graphic Statics, Physical Prototyping, Structure
